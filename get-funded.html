Is your business looking for some extra capital, smart money or funds?

Inventures invest up to €1,000,000 in disruptive start-ups.

Inventures II is a Venture Capital fund aiming for the €50 Million mark and investing capital in smart, innovative and sustainable European scale-ups.

Inventures II is supporting the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs): it aims to support a responsible and sustainable economy in Europe by empowering entrepreneurship through equity investments, mostly in start-ups and scale-ups as they represent 85% of net job creation.

Inventures is working in partnership with MyMicroInvest, a leading investment platform, on various projects reflecting its vision and mission.

We are passionate about helping young companies grow, prosper and succeed, If you're looking for growth capital for your impactful business, Inventures invest from 250,000 and up to €1,000,000 in disruptive start-ups and scale-ups.
