Board & Team

Board

Inventures is managed by MyMicroInvest S.A.. The Inventures investment committee meets approximately once a month to review investments, make decisions, and act as a representative of shareholders and management.

The Board of Directors of Inventures II is composed of the three managing team members plus MyMicroInvest, acting as managing director. Similar to Inventures I, the board has set up a separate organ named the Investment Committee. The Investment Committee is composed of seven directors, plus three other members. The Investment Committee is an independent shareholders representative.

Alongside the Board and the Investment Committee, there is an Advisory Board. It acts as strategic advisor to the Board of Directors for questions related to impact assessment and conflicts of interests. It consists of one MyMicroInvest team member and two independent experts in sustainability and/or corporate governance matters.

Team

Inventures is managed by MyMicroInvest S.A., which is composed of the following team:

Olivier de Duve

Chief Executive Officer

Olivier has 17 years of experience in investment banking, capital markets and investment management in New York, Paris and London with Bear Stearns, Deutsche Bank, IXIS and Shinsei International.

In 2008, he decided to start his own venture with partners. His interest for disruptive technologies led him to become co-founder of Earlytracks S.A. He then launched SemCap, an advisory boutique focused on start-ups and SMEs.

He is also a board member and private investor in various start-ups and is active in the Steering Committee of the “de Duve Institute”, a multidisciplinary biomedical research institute.

He holds a Master of Science in Business engineering degree from the Solvay Business School and is a FSA registered investment advisor.

Gilles van der Meerschen

Premium Clients

For more than 20 years, Gilles has built up a know-how with a strong track record in product marketing, strategic planning, process optimisation and people management. His network extends across all continents giving access to finance in the private company market place, be it hedge funds, high net-worth investors, private equity firms or family offices.

Gilles is a board member of several high profile companies.

Gilles holds a bachelor degree in Business and Administration and a master degree in Marketing from the European Business School of London. A few years later, he followed the Program for Executive Development (PED) at IMD.

Bruno de Radzitzky

Head of Asset Management

Bruno has almost 25 years experience in M&A transactions in Europe, the USA, South America, Asia and South Africa. He started his career as a lawyer, member of the Brussels Bar. He then joined Etex (a Belgian group active in building materials around the world) as a Company Lawyer, and then as Senior Legal Counsel. He joined MyMicroInvest on January 1st 2017 as Head of Asset Management.

He served as a director of several Etex companies in Belgium, Luxembourg, France and Spain. He also was a board member and the Compliance Officer of the Etex Pension Fund.

Bruno holds a Master’s Degree in Laws (Louvain-LN), a post degree in International and European Laws (VUB) and a LLM (Queen Mary & Westfield College).

Inès Mertens de Wilmars

Investment Manager

Inès has over eight years of experience in Business Valuations and Corporate Finance. She spent four years at Deloitte Corporate Finance where she gained her financial expertise in global corporate finance including business valuations, mergers, and acquisitions.

In 2006, she moved to California where she gained extensive experience in business valuation theory and practice. She worked with companies in all stages of development, specifically with early stage companies in the Silicon Valley.

She holds a Master’s degree in business administration from the University of Louvain-la-Neuve (UCL). She has become a member of the National Association of Certified Valuation Analyst (NACVA) and received the designation of Accredited Valuation Analyst (AVA).

Philippine du Parc Locmaria

Investment Analyst

Philippine joined MyMicroInvest in 2016 as an Investment Analyst after completing two internships; as a Junior Investment Analyst at GBL in Brussels and in Mergers and Acquisitions at HSBC in Paris. After different work experiences in a Belgian SMO, Philippine decided to go into the exciting world of entrepreneurship.

Philippine studied in Belgium and China. She post-graduated Magna Cum Laude from Business Engineering at Louvain School of Management (Finance and Management major).

Brice Moureau

Investment Analyst
